Frequently Asked Questions

How long do custom orders take?

Custom orders typically take 2-4 weeks depending on complexity and our current workload. We'll provide you with a more accurate timeframe after reviewing your request.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, we require a 50% deposit to begin work on custom orders. The remaining balance will be due before shipping.

Can I make changes during the process?

Minor adjustments can be made during the initial design phase. Once production has begun, changes may incur additional costs.

What if I'm not satisfied with the final product?

We work closely with customers throughout the process to ensure satisfaction. In the rare event that the product doesn't meet expectations, we'll work with you to make it right.
